The Stand

A real L.A. taco spot.

No frills, no shortcuts — just consistently great tacos, night after night.

Tacos El Pecas works the corner of South Soto Street the way the best L.A. taco stands always have: fresh meat on the comal, warm tortillas, habanero salsa and grilled onions, and regulars who swear it's the best in the county.

It's an after-dark kind of place. Doors open in the late afternoon and we run straight through the evening — later still on weekends. Walk up, order a few, and see what the fuss is about.
